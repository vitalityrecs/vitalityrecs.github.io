Welcome to Vitality Records. Vitality Records is a Drum & Bass record label founded in Kentucky by Zach Fetters for the sole purpose of sharing the best Drum & Bass, Neuro, and Breaks tunes with the world. We started in 2016, but our story goes farther back than that.

In August 13th, 2013, Zach launched a music promotion channel under the name of "Oiram Media", with the sole purpose of spreading the tunes he loved the most at that time. In early 2014, "Oiram Media" transferred into something bigger, a record label. After a good 3 year run, Oiram Media disbanded in January of 2016. In February 2016, Zach started up Vitality Records.

...and more of our story is to come!
