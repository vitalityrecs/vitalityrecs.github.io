Your music will be uploaded and released to Soundcloud, iTunes, Spotify, and all other major streaming and distribution services.

 

All revenue will be collected at the end of each month. If we do not reach a collective 25 Euros or 28.14 US Dollars, the revenue will be carried over to the next month. Otherwise, revenue will be collected at the end of each month, split 50/50 (based on the contracts revenue share agreement), and payed out to your Paypal account.

 

All demos must not have been released in any way, and have all unauthorized samples cleared for release. If more than one artist was involved, please credit them as well.

 

If you are under 18, please let us know so that we can ask a parent or legal guardian for their co-sign on your contract. Failure to notify us will result in the cancellation of your contract and the takedown of your releases.
